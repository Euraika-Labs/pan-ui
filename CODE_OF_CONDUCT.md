# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Expected behavior
- Be kind and assume good intent
- Give clear, actionable feedback
- Focus on improving the project
- Respect different levels of experience

Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

Enforcement
Project maintainers may moderate discussions and contributions to keep the project healthy and productive.
